What Makes This Platform an AI Project

Recommendation Engine

The platform analyzes learner activity, categories, quiz scores, and difficulty preference to rank the next best courses.

Smart Progress Insights

Student dashboards surface AI-generated study priorities, milestone alerts, and learning profile tags from real LMS activity.

Course Match Scoring

Every course can be evaluated against a learner profile to show explainable AI match scores and next-step reasoning.

AI Chatbot Assistant

A floating chatbot answers learner questions about courses, certificates, quizzes, pricing, FAQs, and student progress.
